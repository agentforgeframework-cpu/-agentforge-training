# Training Philosophy

## Core Principle

Teach practical operational understanding through small, understandable systems.

## Educational Goals

AgentForge training is designed to:
- improve operational understanding
- reduce unnecessary complexity
- connect architecture to real workflows
- improve reproducibility and governance
- support real-world usage

## Teaching Style

Training material should:
- remain practical
- avoid unnecessary hype
- focus on operational meaning
- emphasize understanding over memorization
- prefer working examples over abstract theory

## Standards

All training material should:
- be reproducible
- use consistent naming
- use documented folder structure
- support workshop delivery
- support self-study usage

## Distribution Philosophy

Training material is intended for:
- self-study
- user groups
- conference workshops
- internal enterprise training
- educational reuse with attribution
