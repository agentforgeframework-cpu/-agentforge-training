# Additional Resources

AF-002 is intentionally designed as a bounded operational learning experience focused on:
- telemetry
- operational visibility
- SAS-supported investigation
- AI-assisted observation
- Human-in-Command reasoning

The resources below are provided for learners who wish to continue exploring related topics.

External resources are maintained by their respective owners. Inclusion here does not imply endorsement of all viewpoints, architectures, or operational approaches.

AF-002 intentionally prioritizes:
- small understandable operational systems
- operational clarity
- survivability
- restartability
- practical reasoning

over unnecessary complexity or hyperscale infrastructure assumptions.

# Suggested Practice Areas

Learners wishing to continue practicing operational telemetry concepts may explore small understandable operational systems such as:

- parking lot occupancy
- room occupancy tracking
- hallway congestion
- loading dock activity
- garden wildlife observation
- bird feeder telemetry
- warehouse region monitoring
- pedestrian flow observation

The goal is operational understanding and investigation, not architectural complexity.

---

# What AF-002 Does NOT Cover

AF-002 intentionally does not focus on:
- advanced machine learning engineering
- hyperscale orchestration systems
- robotics engineering
- autonomous weapons systems
- enterprise cloud governance
- production cybersecurity engineering
- industrial control systems
- large-scale AI platform operations

The course is intentionally scoped around operational telemetry understanding using approachable, survivable workflows.

---

# Final Note

AF-002 emphasizes:
- operational reasoning
- telemetry interpretation
- survivability
- Human-in-Command workflows
- SAS-supported operational analysis

The goal is not to create dependency on large or complex infrastructure.

The goal is to help learners think clearly about operational evidence and telemetry-driven workflows using systems they can reasonably understand, investigate, explain, and restart.
