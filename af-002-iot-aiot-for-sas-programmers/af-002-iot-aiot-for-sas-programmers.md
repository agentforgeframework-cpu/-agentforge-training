# Purpose

AF-002 is a practical operational course for SAS professionals exploring IoT and AIoT workflows.

The course teaches SAS professionals that their existing operational analytics mindset already applies naturally to telemetry systems, event systems, and AIoT operational workflows.

---

# Core Objective

By the end of AF-002, learners should be able to:

> Capture, process, analyze, and reason about operational telemetry using practical SAS-centered AIoT workflows.

---

# Instructional Philosophy

## Human in Command

Systems may assist operations. Humans retain judgment, accountability, and operational authority.

## Fast First Success

The learner should quickly move from webcam input to CSV data to SAS analysis.

## Action Before Theory

The first success matters more than the first explanation.

## Architecture Follows Data

Workloads should be placed where they naturally function best.

## Lightweight Survivability

The course avoids dependency sprawl, hardware complexity, cloud lock-in, and enterprise over-engineering.

---

# v1.0 Lesson Sequence

## Lesson 00 - Setup and Orientation

Check the learner's environment and establish the study/lab workspace.

## Lesson 01 - Simple Video and Images

Create the first telemetry-to-CSV-to-SAS success.

## Lesson 02 - Event Streams and Real Time Thinking

Move from single samples to operational sequences and events.

## Lesson 03 - AIoT Decision Loops

Connect rules, validation, AI assistance, and operational judgment.

## Lesson 04 - Architecture Follows Data for AIoT

Place telemetry, analysis, and decisions where the workload makes sense.

## Lesson 05 - Capstone Workflow

Apply the course in one small directed telemetry workflow.
