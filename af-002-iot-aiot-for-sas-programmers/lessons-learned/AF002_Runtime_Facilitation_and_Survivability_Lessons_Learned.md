# AF002 Runtime Facilitation and Survivability — Lessons Learned

- The learner became significantly more engaged once acting as an operational investigator instead of an observer.
- Artifact visibility matters as much as artifact existence.
- AI runtime facilitation behavior is itself a deployment surface.
- Visible checkpoints dramatically improve learner survivability and pacing.
- Restartability and clean-room survivability are first-class instructional requirements.
- Human walkthroughs revealed pacing and ambiguity issues earlier than background analysis alone.
- Multi-model digital twin testing surfaced different classes of survivability issues.
