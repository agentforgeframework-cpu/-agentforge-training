* ChatGPT

```text
Use SAS telemetry evidence
```

# Major Discovery

## Operational Realism Alone Is Insufficient

One of the most important findings:

```text
Operational realism can accidentally mask
instructional identity collapse.
```

The lesson became:

* emotionally believable
* organizationally realistic
* operationally authentic

while still failing to:

* center SAS investigation
* create learner operational agency
* produce architecture discovery through SAS evidence

This is considered a portable instructional-engineering finding.

# Architecture Drift Discovery

Architecture repeatedly failed to emerge naturally.

The lesson discussed:

* telemetry
* dashboards
* operational pressure
* modernization

without learners operationally concluding:

```text
architecture decisions created these consequences
```

This revealed an important design principle:

```text
Architecture becomes stable instructionally
when discovered through operational consequences,
not through conceptual discussion.
```

The strongest architecture-learning moments emerged from:

* stale telemetry
* visibility lag
* manual compensation
* reconciliation effort
* dashboard mismatch
* telemetry freshness problems

These operational behaviors naturally led toward architecture reasoning.
