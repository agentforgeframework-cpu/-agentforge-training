# AF002 Operational Reasoning and Telemetry — Lessons Learned

- Dashboards are useful abstractions, not operational truth.
- Timestamp lineage became one of the strongest operational “aha” concepts in the course.
- Reports themselves are telemetry and require interpretation.
- “The answer is in the data — or the lack of the data” emerged as a core operational theme.
- Strong dashboards can still hide operationally critical realities.
- Migration success can unintentionally reduce operational visibility.
- Operational modernization discussions are best framed as proportionality decisions, not ideology.
- Bounding-box reasoning became operationally meaningful only after visible examples and spatial grounding.
