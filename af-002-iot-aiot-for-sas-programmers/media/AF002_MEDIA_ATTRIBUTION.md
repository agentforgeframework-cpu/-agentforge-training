# AI-Generated Media Disclosure

Some AF-002 instructional visuals were generated or AI-assisted during course development.

Generated media should:
- undergo human review
- undergo instructional review
- be operationally validated
- be explicitly approved before learner-facing deployment

This requirement emerged directly from operational testing and survivability validation during AF-002 development.
