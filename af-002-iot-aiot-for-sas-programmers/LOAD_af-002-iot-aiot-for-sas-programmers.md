Do not require the learner to manually run LOAD files during normal use.

Keep the course small, practical, and operational.

Avoid:
- AI hype
- Python-first drift
- cloud obsession
- hardware complexity
- enterprise architecture sprawl
- LMS behavior
- excessive orchestration language

- SAS-centered explanations
- visible operational artifacts
- CSV data
- timestamps
- rows
- trends
- events
- simple rules
- human judgment
