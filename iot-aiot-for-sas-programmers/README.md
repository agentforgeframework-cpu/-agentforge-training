# IoT/AIoT for SAS Programmers

A practical operational introduction to AIoT systems for experienced SAS professionals.

## Audience

- SAS programmers
- SAS administrators
- enterprise analytics professionals
- operational analytics teams

## Philosophy

This course emphasizes:
- operational understanding
- architecture awareness
- realistic systems
- small understandable labs
- event-driven thinking
- integration back into SAS analytics workflows

## Course Structure

1. Simple Video and Images
2. Events, Schemas, and Exception Handling
3. From CSV to SAS Analytics
4. Live Streams vs Batch Images
5. Simple Rules and Operational Meaning
6. Middleware Basics for SAS Programmers
7. AIoT ROI and Architecture Follows Data
8. Building Simple Digital Twins
9. Analytics for AIoT Data with SAS
10. Architecture, Scale, and Operational Systems

## Capstone

Build a Small Operational AIoT Monitoring System
