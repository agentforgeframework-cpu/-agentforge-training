# Expected Learning Style

AF-001 is:
- practical
- conversational
- experiment-driven
- lightweight

You are encouraged to:
- try prompts
- compare results
- refine instructions
- observe behavior
- think critically

This course is not built around:
- memorization
- exams
- certifications
- technical jargon

---

# Important Mindset

AI systems can:
- assist
- organize
- summarize
- generate ideas

Humans remain responsible for:
- judgment
- verification
- decisions
- final use

The goal is not blind trust.

The goal is thoughtful usage.

---

# Recommended Pace

Suggested pace:
- 20–40 minutes per lesson

However:
- learners may move faster
- learners may move slower
- lessons may be revisited later

This course is designed to support practical experimentation rather than rigid pacing.

---

# If You Feel Stuck

That is normal.

Recommended actions:
- simplify the prompt
- ask follow-up questions
- compare multiple responses
- experiment with wording
- continue iterating

AI interaction is often iterative.
